Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?
We pride ourselves on quick response times. Most inquiries receive a response within 24-48 hours during business days. For coaching program inquiries and franchise opportunities, we typically schedule a discovery call within 3-5 business days.
Do you offer virtual consultations?
Absolutely! Clinton conducts coaching sessions and business consultations via secure video conferencing worldwide. This allows us to work with clients regardless of geographic location while maintaining the personal connection that drives results.
What's the best way to get started with your services?
The best first step is to fill out our contact form with details about your goals and interests. This helps us understand your needs and schedule the most appropriate type of initial consultation, whether it's for coaching, franchising, or business consulting.
Do you work with businesses of all sizes?
Yes! Through Clinton's various programs and services, we work with everyone from individual entrepreneurs just starting out to established corporations looking to franchise or optimize their operations. Our MyManager CRM is specifically designed to scale with businesses of all sizes.
How can I support Operation Child Rescue?
We welcome support for our humanitarian work through Operation Child Rescue. You can learn more about our mission, volunteer opportunities, and donation options by selecting "Operation Child Rescue" in your inquiry or visiting our dedicated philanthropy page.
