Our Mission

Founded on the principles of giving and growth, Operation Child Rescue is dedicated to combating child hunger worldwide.

A Foundation Built on Giving

Growing up in a Martial Arts and Christian based home, the concept of giving was always part of my traditions. We started out in our franchise by raising over 100K dollars for St. Judes Children's hospital and many other organizations.

Teaching our community that giving is also part of growth, we established Operation Child Rescue in efforts to give back to the world. Our goal is ambitious but achievable: providing 1 million meals to children all over the world in need.

Operation CR Inc, is a tax-exempt (501c3), not-for-profit corporation organized and operated exclusively for charitable purposes, ensuring every donation directly impacts the lives of children in need.
